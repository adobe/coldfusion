# Adobe Products Overview

## Adobe Creative Cloud
Adobe Creative Cloud is a subscription service giving access to 20+ creative desktop and mobile apps.
- Photoshop: Industry-standard image editing and compositing
- Illustrator: Vector graphics and logo design
- Premiere Pro: Professional video editing
- After Effects: Motion graphics and visual effects
- InDesign: Page layout for print and digital
- XD: UI/UX design and prototyping
- Lightroom: Photo editing and management
Pricing: All Apps plan at $54.99/month (individual) or $89.99/month (business)

## Adobe Acrobat
PDF creation, editing, signing, and sharing. Acrobat Pro DC includes AI-powered tools for summarising, comparing, and generating content in PDFs.
Pricing: Acrobat Pro at $19.99/month

## Adobe Experience Platform (AEP)
Real-time customer data platform. Collects, standardises, and activates customer data across channels.
Features: Real-time CDP, Journey Analytics, Journey Optimizer, Customer AI.

## Adobe ColdFusion
ColdFusion is a rapid web development platform targeting Java enterprise developers.
- ColdFusion 2025 Professional: $1299 one-time license
- ColdFusion 2025 Standard: $799 one-time license
- ColdFusion Developer Edition: Free (limited to 2 IPs and 2 CPUs)
Key features: AI/LLM integration, REST APIs, PDF generation, ORM, WebSockets, Server Monitor

## Adobe Firefly
Adobe's generative AI image model. Trained on licensed and public-domain content.
Available in: Creative Cloud apps, Firefly web app, and via API.
Capabilities: Text-to-image, generative fill, text effects, vector recoloring.
