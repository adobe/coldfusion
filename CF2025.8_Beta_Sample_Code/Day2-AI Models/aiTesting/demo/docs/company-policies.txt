# Company IT and Software Policies

## Software Procurement Policy
All software purchases must be approved by the IT department and budgeted through the departmental expense system.
- Purchases under $500: Manager approval only
- Purchases $500–$5000: Manager + Finance Director approval
- Purchases over $5000: Executive Committee approval required
Preferred vendors: Adobe, Microsoft, Atlassian, AWS, GitHub

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
- Core hours: 10am–3pm local time
- VPN required for all internal system access
- Security training must be completed annually

## Data Retention Policy
- Customer data: Retained for 7 years post contract termination
- Employee records: Retained for 10 years post employment
- Application logs: 90 days rolling retention
- Backups: Taken daily, retained for 30 days

## Password Policy
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Changed every 90 days
- Cannot reuse last 10 passwords
- MFA required for all cloud services

## Support Escalation Process
Level 1: Helpdesk (response within 2 hours, Mon–Fri 8am–6pm)
Level 2: Senior Engineer (response within 4 hours)
Level 3: Architecture Team (response within 1 business day)
Critical/P1 Issues: 24/7 on-call rotation, 30-minute response SLA
