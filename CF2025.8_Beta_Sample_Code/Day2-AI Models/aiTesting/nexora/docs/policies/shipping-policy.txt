NEXORA — SHIPPING POLICY
Effective: January 1, 2026

STANDARD SHIPPING
Free on all orders over $50.
Delivery in 3-5 business days after order is processed.
Orders placed before 2:00 PM PST ship same business day (Monday–Friday).
Orders placed after 2:00 PM PST ship the next business day.
Standard rate: $5.99 for orders under $50.

EXPRESS SHIPPING OPTIONS
2 Business Days: $12.99
Next Business Day (order by 1:00 PM PST): $24.99
Saturday Delivery: $34.99 (available in select areas)

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
Canada: 5-10 business days — $19.99
United Kingdom and Europe: 7-14 business days — $29.99
Australia and New Zealand: 10-16 business days — $34.99
Other countries: Contact support@nexora.com for rates and availability.

ORDER PROCESSING TIMES
Most orders process within 1 business day.
Custom or personalized orders: 3-5 business days before shipping.
Pre-ordered items ship on the listed release date.
Backordered items: You will receive an email with an updated estimated ship date.

TRACKING YOUR ORDER
A tracking number is emailed to you within 24 hours of shipment.
Track via the carrier's website or the Nexora order status page.
SMS tracking updates are available — opt in during checkout or in your account settings.

DELIVERY ISSUES
If your package shows "Delivered" but has not been received, contact us within 5 days.
We will file a carrier claim and ship a replacement (or issue a full refund) within 3 business days.
Nexora is not responsible for packages stolen after confirmed delivery.
For high-value orders, we recommend requiring a signature at delivery — select this option at checkout.
