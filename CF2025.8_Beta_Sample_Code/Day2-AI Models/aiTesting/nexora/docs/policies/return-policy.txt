NEXORA — RETURN POLICY
Effective: January 1, 2026

PHYSICAL PRODUCTS
You may return any physical product within 30 days of the delivery date.
Items must be in original, unopened packaging with all accessories included.
Proof of purchase (order number) is required for all returns.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item.
Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items.
Customer is responsible for return shipping on change-of-mind returns ($9.99 flat rate label provided).

NON-RETURNABLE PHYSICAL ITEMS
- Personalized or custom-engraved products
- Perishable goods
- Items explicitly marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Hazardous materials

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
Report damaged or defective items within 48 hours of delivery via chat, phone, or email.
We will ship a replacement at no cost, or issue a full refund — your choice.
For orders under $50, you do not need to return the defective item.

EXCHANGES
Free exchanges for a different color or size within 30 days.
Size exchanges must be initiated within 14 days of delivery.

HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
Contact support via live chat, email (returns@nexora.com), or phone 1-800-NEXORA.
You will receive a Return Authorization (RA) number and a prepaid shipping label within 24 hours.
Ship the item within 7 days of receiving your RA number.
