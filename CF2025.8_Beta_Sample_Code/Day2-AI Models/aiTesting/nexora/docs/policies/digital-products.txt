NEXORA — DIGITAL PRODUCTS POLICY
Effective: January 1, 2026

WHAT COUNTS AS A DIGITAL PRODUCT
The following are considered digital products under this policy:
- Software licenses and activation keys
- Downloadable applications, games, or programs
- eBooks and digital publications
- Digital gift cards and store credit codes
- Subscription services and access pass codes
- Streaming content passes and media licenses

REFUND POLICY — IMPORTANT
Digital products are strictly non-refundable once any of the following has occurred:
- The license key or activation code has been revealed or accessed
- The download has started
- The account has been activated or the subscription period has begun

This no-refund policy applies regardless of whether the product has been used.

INSTALLATION ISSUES AND TECHNICAL PROBLEMS
If your digital download will not install, fails to activate, or does not work after installation, this qualifies as a technical defect.
Contact our support team within 72 hours of purchase — we will troubleshoot the issue at no cost.
If our support team cannot resolve the installation or activation problem, we will issue a full refund.
You do not need to "return" a digital download — contact support and request a refund under the technical defect exception.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS — DIGITAL PRODUCTS
Q: Can I return a digital download that won't install?
A: Yes — a digital download that will not install is treated as a technical defect. Contact support within 72 hours; if we cannot fix it, you will receive a full refund. You do not need to physically return anything.

EXCEPTIONS WHERE A REFUND MAY BE CONSIDERED
We may issue a refund at our sole discretion in the following limited situations:
1. You were charged but never received the download link or license key.
2. The product is technically defective and our support team is unable to resolve the issue (including installation failures).
3. You were charged twice for the same item due to a billing error.

To request an exception, contact us within 72 hours of purchase with your order number and a description of the issue. Our team reviews all exception requests within 1 business day.

SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES
Cancel your subscription anytime — no cancellation fees.
Access to the service continues until the end of your current paid billing period.
No refunds are issued for partial billing periods.
To cancel, go to Account Settings or contact support.
Auto-renewal can be disabled in your account settings at any time.

DIGITAL GIFT CARDS AND STORE CREDIT
Non-refundable and non-transferable.
Valid for 3 years from the date of issue.
Lost, stolen, or compromised gift card codes cannot be replaced.
