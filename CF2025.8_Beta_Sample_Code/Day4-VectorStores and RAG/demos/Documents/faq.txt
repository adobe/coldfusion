ABC Platform — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address
and you will receive a password reset link within 5 minutes. The link expires after 1 hour.
If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support@abc.io.

Q: Can I export my project data?
A: Yes. Go to Project Settings > Export and choose your format (CSV, JSON, or PDF).
CSV exports include all tasks with their fields. JSON exports include the full project
structure. PDF exports generate a formatted project report. Exports are available on
all paid plans.

Q: What happens when I exceed my storage limit?
A: You will receive a warning notification at 80% and 95% usage. Once you reach 100%,
you can still access existing files but cannot upload new ones. You can free up space
by deleting unused attachments or upgrade to a higher plan for more storage.

Q: How does the billing work?
A: Billing is per-user, per-month. When you add a new user mid-cycle, you are charged
a prorated amount for the remaining days in the billing period. When you remove a user,
the credit is applied to your next invoice. Annual billing is available at a 20% discount.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are
SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data is stored in AWS data centers with
99.99% uptime SLA. Backups are taken every 6 hours and retained for 30 days.
Enterprise customers can choose their data region (US, EU, or Asia-Pacific).

Q: Can I use ABC offline?
A: The mobile apps support limited offline access. You can view tasks and add comments
while offline. Changes are synced automatically when you reconnect. The web application
requires an internet connection at all times.

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Account Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This action is irreversible.
All your data, projects, and files will be permanently deleted after a 30-day grace period.
During the grace period, you can contact support to cancel the deletion.

Q: What integrations are available?
A: We integrate with Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, Jira, Microsoft Teams, Zapier, and
Salesforce. We also provide a REST API for building custom integrations. API documentation
is available at docs.abc.io/api.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Email support@abc.io for all plans. Live chat is available for Team plan and above
during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST, Monday to Friday). Phone support is available
for Enterprise customers only. Average response time is under 4 hours for paid plans.
