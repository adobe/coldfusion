ABC Inc. — Employee Handbook

Section 1: Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to the following paid time off:
- Annual Leave: 20 days per calendar year, accrued monthly at 1.67 days/month
- Sick Leave: 12 days per year. A medical certificate is required for 3 or more consecutive days
- Personal Leave: 3 days per year for personal matters
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 4 weeks for secondary caregiver
- Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family members

Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance.
Emergency leave can be requested with same-day approval from your direct manager.
Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year.
Any leave beyond the carry-forward limit will be forfeited on January 1st.

Section 2: Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval and an updated employment agreement.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local timezone.
All employees must be available during core hours for meetings and communications.
A stable internet connection of at least 25 Mbps download speed is required.
Company-issued equipment must be used for all work-related activities.
VPN connection is mandatory when accessing internal systems from outside the office.

Section 3: Expense Reimbursement

Employees may claim reimbursement for approved business expenses:
- Travel: Economy class flights for domestic, business class for flights over 6 hours
- Hotels: Standard rooms up to $200 per night ($300 in high-cost cities)
- Meals: Up to $75 per day during business travel
- Software & Tools: Pre-approved purchases up to $500/year without additional approval
- Training & Development: Up to $2,500 per year for conferences, courses, and certifications
- Home Office: One-time $1,000 setup allowance for remote employees

All expense claims must include itemized receipts and be submitted within 30 days.
Claims exceeding $500 require manager pre-approval before the expense is incurred.
Reimbursements are processed on the 15th of each month.

Section 4: Code of Conduct

All employees must maintain professional and respectful behavior in all interactions.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are strictly prohibited.
Confidential company information must not be shared externally without authorization.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to your manager and the compliance team.
Company resources should be used for business purposes only.
Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
